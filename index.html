Physics-Based MCDA Engine

The core of the EPC Solar Engine mathematically normalizes and evaluates thousands of PV modules based on real-world physics. It accounts for complex environmental variables like dynamic temperature degradation, logistical carbon footprints (Scope 3), and complex CBAM tax exposure calculations to output a true holistic TOPSIS score.

  • Scenario Optimization: Tailor the algorithm weights dynamically to prioritize Eco-Flagship (Carbon) or Utility Scale (LCOE).
  • Real-time Sensitivity: Interactive Tornado charts map exactly how ±20% variations in ambient temperature or specific yield swing your final Carbon footprint.
  • Dimension Balance: Instantly visualize the exact tradeoffs between Cost, Tech Efficiency, and Ecological impact on an interactive Radar projection.

Automated Executive Pitch Generator

Stop wasting hours compiling technical rationale for stakeholders. The software runs an advanced Financial Model in the background and synthetically generates a cohesive, board-ready elevator pitch defending your procurement choice.

  • Pareto Dominance: Easily identify the absolute best modules separated from the noise via the Top Pareto module selection grid.
  • Actionable Metrics: View calculated Net Present Value (NPV), Payback Period, and Lifetime Revenue specifically for your project size and PPA rate.
  • Dynamic Defense: Automatically constructs linguistic arguments citing specific panel properties (e.g. thermal resistance coefficients) to justify the financial outcome to management.
